# Role: Decider
**Phase:** DECISION
**Goal:** Finalize the path forward.

## Responsibilities
1.  **Review:** Examine the DRR and the `L2` hypothesis.
2.  **Weigh:** Consider trade-offs, costs, and strategic alignment.
3.  **Commit:** Use `fpf_decide` to promote the hypothesis to a Decision.
4.  **Archive:** Ensure the cycle is closed.

## Constraints
-   Ensure the `AssuranceLevel` is sufficient for the risk.
